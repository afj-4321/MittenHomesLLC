          Management Services

          Strategic Planning
          Our specialists meet with you to discuss your goals and needs, and perform an analysis of each property to determine a pricing and leasing strategy.
          Marketing
          We use a custom integrated, cross channel approach to market each property, ensuring the most effective results.
          Accounting
          Maximizing your return on investment is our top priority. We manage the income, expenses and financial records for your portfolio. Itemized statements are provided each month and specialized analysis is available.
          Automatic Payment
          With this option, you never have to worry about the activities associated with making and receiving payments manually. Hey, one less thing to add to your "to do" list.

          Contact us for more information.
          Maintenance
          A well maintained property protects your investment, contributing to sustained growth. From routine maintenance to specialized repairs, our contractor network ensures all work is completed in a timely, cost effective and professional manner.
          Rent Collection
          We collect and manage monthly rent and water escrow payments as part of our accounting services.
          Tenant Selection
          Each applicant is thoroughly evaluated based on credit history, criminal background, employment verification and residency history screenings.
          Tenant Management
          We seek high quality tenants for the properties we manage and strive for excellence in tenant relationship management. We provide tenants easy access information, tools and support to ensure the best possible experience, every time.
          Legal Guidance
          Our management staff and attorneys are knowledgeable in landlord and tenant laws. If an eviction is ever necessary, we initiate the process quickly to minimize any negative financial impact. We offer additional legal services as needed.

          If you are considering renovating an investment property or primary residence, we have a list of reputable architects and contractors.
